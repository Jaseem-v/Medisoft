About Medisoft

Established in 1989, DIGITRONICS our parent company was engaged in the sales and services of Modern Banking Equipment. Understanding the ever-changing market, After 10 years of success in the industry, in 1999, a new IT Division was established. MEDISOFT, one of India's Best Leading Pharmacy Solution was born out of it.

At DIGITRONICS, we're not just creating software. We understand our clients and provide solutions that help them excel in their industry. We value Honesty and Integrity. As a result of that, we have developed good relationships with our clients and are now one of the Major Service-Based Organizations in the Country.

Understanding the need for a Pharmaceutical Solution in the market, MEDISOFT was created. It is the Crown Jewel of our Organization.It provides a Comprehensive Solution for pharmaceutical retail and wholesale establishments and has been recognized for its reliable and user-friendly approach even for the challenging requirements. From Inventory Management to Sales and Billing, MEDISOFT provides a One-Touch Billing System. And it is backed by an up-to-date and comprehensive database of the pharmaceutical products in the market. With the trust of over 2500 Customers, and with the help of a dedicated and passionate group of employees, MEDISOFT is growing day by day.
